AOU SUPPORT CHATBOT - FAQ

Q1: How can I reset my LMS password?
A1: Visit the AOU LMS login page and click on “Forgot Password”, then follow the instructions.

Q2: Can I access the chatbot without logging in?
A2: Yes, you can use the chatbot as a guest, but login is required to save chat history or export sessions.

Q3: What kind of questions can I ask?
A3: You can ask about registration, course schedules, GPA calculation, or general university policies.

Q4: How do I export my conversation?
A4: Click the “Export Chat” button and choose PDF or TXT format.

Q5: Is the chatbot available on mobile devices?
A5: Yes, it is fully responsive and works on both desktop and mobile browsers.

Q6: How does the chatbot understand my questions?
A6: It uses the OpenAI API to generate intelligent and context-aware responses.
